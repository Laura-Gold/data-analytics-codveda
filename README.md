🚀 **Data Analytics Portfolio – Codveda Tasks**
📌 **Overview**

This repository captures my hands-on journey through a structured Data Analytics program, where I progressed from foundational analysis to advanced machine learning and business intelligence.

Across three levels, I worked with real-world datasets to uncover insights, build predictive models, and translate data into actionable business decisions.
The focus of this project goes beyond technical execution, it emphasizes on problem-solving, analytical thinking, and value-driven insights.

🧭 **Project Structure**
🔹 **Level 1: Foundational Analysis**

At this stage, the focus was on understanding the structure and quality of the data, including:

-Performing Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on the Churn Prediction dataset
-Cleaning and preparing data to improve reliability and usability
-Creating basic visualizations to highlight key trends and behavioral patterns

👉 Outcome: Established a solid analytical foundation and identified early indicators of customer churn.
👉 **Outcome**: Developed a strong understanding of customer behavior and key variables influencing churn.

**🔹 Level 2: Intermediate Analytics**

This phase focused on uncovering deeper patterns and segmenting data to drive more targeted insights, including:

-Time Series Analysis on the Stock Prices dataset to identify trends, seasonality, and fluctuations over time
-Customer Segmentation using K-Means Clustering on the Churn Prediction dataset
-Pattern discovery to better understand customer behavior and group similarities

**👉 Outcome**: Revealed meaningful customer segments and time-based trends, enabling more targeted and strategic decision-making.

**🔹 Level 3: Advanced Analytics & Machine Learning**

At this stage, the focus shifted to predictive modeling and translating insights into business impact, including:

-Building and evaluating predictive models on the Churn Prediction dataset:
     1. Logistic Regression
     2. Random Forest (with hyperparameter tuning)
     3. Performing Feature Importance Analysis to identify key drivers of churn
-Developing an interactive Power BI dashboard to communicate insights effectively

**👉 Outcome**: Delivered a predictive framework for identifying high-risk customers and provided actionable insights to support proactive retention strategies.

**📊 Dashboard Highlights (Power BI)**

An interactive dashboard was developed to translate insights into business-friendly visuals:

-KPI Cards: Churn Rate, Total Customers, High-Risk Customers
-Churn Driver Analysis
-Customer Segmentation Insights
-Interactive Filters (e.g., Churn Status, Risk Segment)

**👉 Impact**: Enables stakeholders to quickly identify risk areas and take data-driven actions.

**🔍 Key Insights**
-Customer churn is strongly influenced by pricing sensitivity, service quality, and usage patterns
-Distinct customer segments exhibit different churn behaviors, requiring tailored strategies
-Predictive models can effectively identify high-risk customers before churn occurs
-Data-driven interventions can significantly improve customer retention and lifetime value
